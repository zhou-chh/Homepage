Part 1: Bio & CV

I'm a social psychologist on a mission to create more inclusive and supportive workplaces, especially for LGBTQ+ employees. My work dives deep into how identity disclosure shapes perceptions of professionalism and collaboration.

I'm a responsible team player with an analytical mindset. I enjoy learning and exploring new experiences while collaborating with others to achieve meaningful goals.
